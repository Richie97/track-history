The service

Track Evolution lets you record track-day events, sessions, lap times and notes, chart your progress, import telemetry, and optionally publish a read-only share page. It's free to use and requires signing in with a Google account.

Your content

Everything you log stays yours. By using the service you give us permission to store and process that data for one purpose: operating the service and showing your logbook back to you. Nothing you record is made public unless you enable a share page — and then only the stats a share page includes, under the slug you chose.

Acceptable use

  • attempt to access another user's logbook or account;
  • disrupt, overload, probe or interfere with the service;
  • choose a share slug that impersonates someone else or misleads;
  • enter content you don't have the right to store or publish.
