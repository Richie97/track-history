Your track days, remembered lap by lap.

Track History is a self-hosted HPDE / track-day logbook: events, sessions, lap times and notes per track, with progress charts that show you getting faster season over season. It runs on Cloudflare's free tier and your data stays yours.

Get started View on GitHub

Built around how track days actually work

Tracks contain events, events contain sessions, sessions contain laps — the same shape as your weekend.

Progress charts

Hand-rolled SVG charts of best laps over time per track and layout, plus lap-by-lap overlays to compare sessions. No chart library, no build step.

Telemetry import

Drop in Corvette PDR video, GoPro footage, Racelogic VBO or Garmin FIT files and get sessions with laps. Parsing happens entirely in your browser — videos are read by byte range and never uploaded.

Lap stats that mean something

Best time per event, consistency as coefficient of variation, best-N averages, pace trends and warmup analysis — computed from your actual laps.

Share links

Publish a read-only page of your stats and times under a slug you choose. Notes, email and per-lap details are stripped — share the pace, keep the diary private.

Installable PWA

Add it to your phone's home screen and browse your logbook in the paddock — static assets and recent API responses work offline.

Yours, for free

One Cloudflare Worker plus a D1 SQLite database — comfortably inside the free tier. Google sign-in, and every row is scoped to your user. Multi-user out of the box.

From memory card to logbook

GPS-only recordings have no lap markers — so the import preview draws the track map you actually drove, and you click where the start/finish line is. Laps are timed on every pass, and one picked line applies to the whole batch of files.

Corvette PDR (Cosworth) MP4

Lap times recovered from beacon and odometer telemetry — reverse-engineered and validated against Cosworth Toolbox, including crossings the recorder dropped. Recordings with no beacons at all still get lap times, recovered from the telemetry's lap-to-lap periodicity.

GoPro MP4

The GPS trace from the GPMF metadata track (Hero 5 and newer), timed with the start/finish line picker.

Racelogic VBO

VBOX files and RaceChrono / TrackAddict / Harry's LapTimer exports — laps from the file's own start line when present, otherwise from GPS.

Garmin FIT

Catalyst session exports — the device's own lap messages, or the GPS trace when a file has none.

Self-host it in an afternoon

No servers to run and nothing to pay for. Four steps and it's live on workers.dev (or your own domain).

Create the database

wrangler d1 create track-history and drop the database ID into wrangler.jsonc.

Migrate & seed

Apply the schema, then optionally import your spreadsheet-era history with the seed generator.

Add Google OAuth

Create an OAuth client, point it at /auth/callback, and set the two secrets.

Deploy

npm run deploy. Sign in and your seeded history is claimed automatically.
